# CEO-of-your-life-_-Decision-Board-Room
Your life, run like a boardroom. Bring a real decision, hear from distinct AI directors, respond as CEO, and see how your choices evolve. Track which perspectives you engage or overrule, uncover blind spots, and get a final memo with clear tradeoffs, risks, and direction.

Make decisions like a CEO.

**CEO of Your Life** turns your biggest choices into a boardroom conversation. Instead of overthinking alone, you bring a decision and get perspectives from a full “board of directors” — then respond, challenge them, and see what your behavior reveals over time.

---

## 🚀 What It Does

- Input a real decision you're facing  
- Get perspectives from 5 distinct “directors”:
  - The Skeptic (risk & downside)
  - The Visionary (long-term upside)
  - The Pragmatist (execution reality)
  - The Compass (values & identity)
  - The Devil’s Advocate (contrarian view)

- Respond as the CEO to each director  
- Directors can follow up and challenge you  
- Directors can respond to each other (boardroom dynamic)  
- Receive a **Final Board Memo** with:
  - Recommended direction
  - Tradeoffs
  - Risks & counterpoints

---

## 🧠 What Makes It Different

This is not a chatbot.  
It’s a **behavior mirror**.

Over time, the app tracks:
- Which directors you **agree with**
- Which ones you **overrule**
- Which ones you **engage with most**

This reveals your:
- Decision-making patterns  
- Blind spots  
- Personal “leadership style”  

---

## 🔍 Core Insight

The most important voice isn’t the one you hear.  
It’s the one you consistently ignore.

## ⚡ How to Run

### Option 1 — Easiest (No Setup)
1. Download `board-of-directors-v3.html`
2. Double-click to open in your browser
3. Start using immediately

---

### Option 2 — With OpenAI (Recommended)
1. Get an API key from https://platform.openai.com
2. Paste your key into the app
3. Click **Convene the Board**

---

## ⚠️ No API Key?

No problem.

- The app includes a **smart offline mode**
- It generates tailored responses based on your decision
- You’ll see a confirmation popup before continuing

Perfect for demos, hackathons, or unreliable WiFi.
